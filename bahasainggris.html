Bahasa Inggris

A fun experience in Pentingsari Village

The environment of Hotel Novotel Solo, built in 1997, is cozy with some greenery and a city view. In my opinion, this hotel has a high rating because of its elegant and comfortable interior. The design feels classic yet modern. I especially like the bedroom interior because it looks neat and relaxing. For breakfast, the hotel has a restaurant that serves nasi liwet, bread, fruits, egg station, rice, and various meats. The restaurant was quite busy, especially with many Santa Laurensia students around. It has good facilities like many seats, AC, wifi, and friendly service.
